#include <identifier.hpp>

AccurateRip-Identifier of a compact disc.

Contents

  • Reference
    • Constructors, destructors, conversion operators
    • Public functions

ARId determines the URL of the compact disc dataset as well as the standard filename of the AccurateRip response.
